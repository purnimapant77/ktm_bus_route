What is KTM Bus Finder?

KTM Bus Finder is a free online platform that helps people in Kathmandu Valley find the right public bus for their journey. Simply select your starting stop and destination, and we'll tell you exactly which bus to take, where to board, and where to get off.

We cover major Yatayat services operating across Kathmandu, Lalitpur, and Bhaktapur with accurate route and stop information.
